# Product Requirements Document (PRD) - Septuagint Studies Researchers Network

## 1. Overview
The **Septuagint Studies Researchers Network (SSRN)** is a high-end, minimalist networking platform designed for PhD candidates and established scholars in the field of Septuagint research. The platform avoids "social media clutter" in favor of a traditional, prestigious academic aesthetic inspired by the University of Oxford.

## 2. Core Design Philosophy
- **Oxford Traditional**: Deep blue palette (`#002147`), gold accents, and serif typography (EB Garamond).
- **Minimalist Structure**: Focus on content and research rather than social interactions (no likes, no hearts).
- **Institutional Authority**: The UI conveys prestige, stability, and academic rigor.

## 3. Functional Requirements (Current MVP)

### A. Academic Billboard (Home)
The central hub of the network. Instead of a social feed, it functions as a **Research Publication Feed**.
- **Features**:
  - Display of recent research papers, working papers, and conference abstracts.
  - Author attribution linked to profiles.
  - Call for Papers (CFP) and major academic announcements.
  - Minimal search/filter by research topic.

### B. Researchers Directory
A curated database of all members in the network.
- **Features**:
  - Grid view of researchers with headshots and institutional affiliation.
  - Quick access to scholarly portfolios.
  - Search by name, university, or specific research interest (e.g., Lexicography, Textual Criticism).

### C. Academic Profile (Portfolio)
The researcher's personal identity on the platform.
- **Features**:
  - **Biography**: Detailed research focus.
  - **Recent Publications**: A mirrored view of what appears on the Billboard for that specific user.
  - **Portfolio**: Direct links to download PDF/Word documents.
  - **Manuscript Gallery**: Visual display of manuscripts or lab work.
  - **Affiliation**: Official university department info.

### D. Authentication (Fictitious/Static)
- **Login**: Elegant entry point for registered scholars.
- **Registration**: Onboarding process for new researchers, emphasizing academic credentials.

## 4. Technical Specification
- **Tech Stack**: Static HTML5, Vanilla CSS3, Lucide Icons (CDN).
- **Responsive Design**: Optimized for desktop and tablets (primary academic usage).
- **Assets**: High-resolution images generated specifically for Septuagint and Oxford contexts.

## 5. Future Roadmap
- **Communication System**: Private peer-to-peer messaging for research collaboration.
- **Resource Repository**: A shared database for high-resolution manuscript scans.
- **Grant Tracking**: A dashboard for tracking and applying to Septuagint-specific research grants.
